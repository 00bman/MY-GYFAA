About Us It's Nice to Meet You!

The Greater Fayetteville Apartment Association was established in 2016. We serve the following counties: Cumberland, Hoke, Lee, Moore, Robeson, Scotland, The Greater Fayetteville Apartment Association is a local affiliate of the Apartment Association of North Carolina, and the National Apartment Association. Click on the logos to visit their websites.

2018 Board of Directors and Staff

Catina Rhinehart President

Catina Rhinehart is the current President of the GFAA. Catina is the Operations Manager of Morganton Management, and has been with this company since 2013, with a total of 15 years of experience in our industry. ​Catina has a North Carolina Real Estate Broker's License, both CAM and CAPS designations, and is a NAAEI Faculty Member. She also is the Vice Chair of the City-County Joint Appearance Committee, the Secretary on the Dogwood Festival Board, and an Intake Officer for the Tarheel Weimaraner Rescue.

Jennifer Pittman Vice President

Jennifer previously served the GFAA as the Education Co-Chair, and has earned the CAM designation, and is a NAAEI Proctor. She loves spending time with her family and friends, and anything beach related. She believes strongly in our industry, and in furthering one's education.

Angela Raupp Education

Angela Raupp is the Education Chairperson of the GFAA, and the Community Manager of The Regency ​ Apartments and of the Lakeshore Grande Apartments for Apartment Dynamics. Angela has been with Apartment ​ Dynamics for two years, with over 15 years of experience in our industry total. Angela has earned her NALP, CAM and CAPS designations, and is a Certified Pool Operator. She is also an ​ AANC Leadership Lyceum Graduate, a NAAEI CAM and CAPS proctor and the Class of 2018's 40 under 40 ​ Award Recipient. Angela loves to enjoy life by spending time with her spouse and three children. In her free time, she works out and runs for her IRUN4 buddy. She has participated in four 4K's, one 10 miler, and is training for her first half marathon.

Brenda Leidholdt Membership

Brenda Leidholdt is the Membership Co-Chair for the GFAA. She has been with Wellington Advisors for five years, ​ currently as a Regional Manager, and has twelve years of experience in our industry. ​ Brenda has earned the NALP and CAM designations, and also holds a North Carolina Real Estate Broker License. ​ Brenda enjoys spending time with her family and taking trips to the beach. Her favorite one is Busco Beach so she ​ can ride ATV's and dirt bikes.
